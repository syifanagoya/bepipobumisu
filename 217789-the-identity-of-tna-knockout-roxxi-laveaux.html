The Identity Of TNA Knockout Roxxi Laveaux

Publish date: 2024-03-16


Lance Jr. sent this along:

I wanted to pass along the identity of “Roxxi Laveaux” who debut with the Voodoo Kin Mafia last night at the TNA PPV. She is a local wrestler from New Hampshire who wrestles frequently in the New England area as Nikki Roxx. She was the World Women’s Wrestling (WWW) champion for several months, which is part of the New England Championship Wrestling (NECW) promotion based in the Boston, MA area. She has also appeared in Shimmer, WEW, and other indy wrestling promotions.

She is a very talented women’s wrestler and it should be interesting if TNA decides to use her in an actual wrestling capacity or just as a run-in basis.
