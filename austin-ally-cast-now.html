Austin and Ally Cast: Where Are They Now?

Publish date: 2024-05-04

Let’s take a look at where the Austin and Ally cast are now!

Starting in December 2011, Austin and Ally quickly became one of the most popular Disney Channel shows. The show ended in January 2016 and we thought we’d take a look at what the cast are up to now.

It’s funny aspects and heart warming stories drew thousands of people in to watch. The popular show is still watched by many today despite it being almost a year since it ended. As well as the comedy aspect, Austin and Ally was known for it’s music. Ross Lynch being in the series was a huge perk as he has a very musical background with R5.

Ross Lynch (Austin Moon)

Since Austin and Ally ended, Ross Lynch has been working on new music with R5. As well as working on new music, the band have been touring all around the world. He is also rumoured to be in two films coming in 2017.

Laura Marano (Ally Dawson)

Since Austin and Ally has ended, Laura has been involved in a few projects. She’s been in three music videos, a television film and is rumoured to be in a couple of films next year.

Raini Rodriguez (Trish)

Raini hasn’t actually appeared in anything since Austin and Ally ended in January. But, in the past she has also appeared in ‘Paul Blart: Mall Cop 1 & 2.’ She also appeared in an episode of ‘The Suite Life of Zack & Cody.’ We all hope that Raini will appear on our screens again soon!

Calum Worthy (Dez)

Since the show finished in January and since then Calum has appeared in a couple of things. He appeared in the Thriller ‘The Thinning.’ Also, he appeared in an episode of Bizaardvark called ‘The Collab.’
