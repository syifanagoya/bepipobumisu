Who Is BTB Savage Girlfriend? 

According to the Interview, BTB Savage’s girlfriend is the woman who killed the intruder as a self-defense measure to save BTB’s family and fortune. 

BTB lived with his current girlfriend and his 4-year-old son in San Antonio, Texas, and the robbery took place at their House when all his family members were present. 

Although BTB Savage did not disclose his girlfriend’s name in the interview session with DJ Vlad, he referred to her as the “true gangster.”

Furthermore, BTB added that although his girlfriend had to shoot a person in his direction, she had saved the life of him and his son.

BTB’s son was asleep next door when the gunfight took place in his room, and he heartwarmingly said, “My son, all I got” in the talk show.

Additionally, BTB said he wanted to keep his son oblivious about the incident so he wouldn’t get traumatized. 

Many people who watched the interview criticized harshly yet reasonably the tone BTB was using to describe his girlfriend, who saved his life during the tragic incident. (he was calling her a b*tch in the interview)

One commenter in the video said, “I’m glad he’s alive for his son. I just can’t get over that he would keep referring to this woman as a b*tch as if she didn’t help save his life. Where is the respect?! Smh”

DEA officers questioned BTB Savage’s girlfriend after killing a robber in their home. She was released as it was considered an act of self-defense due to the presence of a child in the House.

BTB Savage Robbery And Shooting Case

In the interview, BTB Savage talked about the night he killed a man in self-defense and explained that it started with a collaboration request from another artist who offered to pay him for a feature.

The rapper stated that he became suspicious when his potential collaborator and their group came into his House and started inspecting it.

After a short introduction, another artist’s group member talked about his chain and threatened the Cleveland rapper and his girlfriend with violence if he didn’t hand it over.

During the altercation, both BTB Savage and the intruder were shot, but BTB Savage survived after he instructed his girlfriend to shoot and kill the intruder. The incident started as a wrestling match for the intruder’s firearms.

BTB Savage further recounts how he managed to guide his son and girlfriend while dealing with his injuries, including a broken and bleeding arm. He also remembers the friend who drove him to the hospital.

In another part of his recollection, BTB Savage remembers waking up in a hospital bed and being interrogated by the authorities regarding the death that took place.

DJ Vlad also shared a similar experience of a home invasion attempt in which he almost had to defend himself towards the end of the video clip.

BTB Savage Death: Who Killed BTB Savage?

It is said words carry more weight than one can possibly understand. Words, once spoken, can never be taken back, and the speaker must be ready to face the consequences if they arise. BTB Savage’s tragic demise serves as an example of this statement.

The late rapper had just started his career and had a long way ahead. Unfortunately, he passed away untimely before he could soar high in the music industry. Born Darrell Gentry, he was shot multiple times on Thursday after 6 pm.

BTB Savage was found dead alongside bullet-riddled Mercedes in the 2100 block of Mid Lane and San Felipe Street in River Oaks. The cops speculate the rapper’s death to be a “targeted” attack. And it has been reported that two individuals in a dark-colored SUV shot him to death.

The incident occurred after the late musician detailed the story of how he and his girlfriend killed a robber who attempted to steal their belongings. He claimed it was a self-defense act and didn’t show fear for the gang’s retaliation for their member’s death.

Although Darrell was arrested for his actions, he was released due to a lack of proof. As his murder happened a few days after the interview, many people believe it was related to the incident. Many assume BTB Savage’s killers to be the gang who killed him to avenge their dead mate.

Furthermore, BTB Savage’s mother, Bernita Ward, has spoken that he was killed in a targeted shooting. She claims her late son’s death was associated with the group who attempted to rob his San Antonio house in February. She asserts it was retaliation for the thief’s death.

Bernita had also warned her son to take off images from social media where he was seen standing in a pool of dried blood before a door that was shot through with bullet holes. She had seen comments about people taunting BTB and talking about killing him.

Although Ward told her kid to let it go, those threats became true. She says,

I said Don’t respond. Let it go. But their family member came to rob my son.

It is unfathomable what Bernita is going through after losing her son. BTB Savage was a son, a father, and an inspiring rapper with massive potential. Sadly, he is no more; the rap industry has lost one of its gems.
