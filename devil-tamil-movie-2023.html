Devil Tamil Movie (2023): Devil is an upcoming Tamil movie directed by Aathityaa and starring Vidharth, Mysskin, Poorna, and Subhashree in the lead roles. The movie is a thriller that revolves around a mysterious serial killer who targets women and leaves behind a signature mark of a devil. The movie is expected to release in theatres on December 15, 2023.

Devil Tamil Movie (2023) Release Date

The movie is scheduled to hit the screens on December 15, 2023. The movie will be released in Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Kerala and other parts of India. The movie will also be released in overseas markets like Singapore, Malaysia, Sri Lanka, UAE, USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and other countries where there is a significant Tamil population. The movie will be released in around 2000 screens worldwide. The movie has been certified with a U/A certificate by the censor board. The movie has a runtime of 139 minutes.
