About Jade Cargill

Jade Cargill, a rising star in the world of professional wrestling, is celebrated for her extraordinary athleticism, formidable presence, and captivating charisma. With a background as a former college basketball player, Jade seamlessly transitioned into the wrestling world, bringing a unique blend of strength, agility, and competitive spirit to the ring.

Having made her debut in All Elite Wrestling (AEW) in 2020, Jade swiftly rose to prominence, amassing a dedicated fanbase and leaving an indelible mark on the promotion. However, in a significant move, she parted ways with AEW in 2023, marking the end of a memorable chapter in her career.

In October of 2023, Jade Cargill embarked on a new journey by joining WWE, one of the most prestigious wrestling organizations in the world. Her transition to WWE promises an exciting future filled with remarkable moments and unforgettable matches.
