Jenny McCarthy: No Makeup, No Recognition?

Publish date: 2024-02-27

Jenny McCarthy has gone makeup-free on Twitter.

At least we think this is Jenny McCarthy.

To her credit, The View co-host gave followers a look at her face prior to sitting in a stylist’s chair this morning, posing for a picture online that makes it nearly impossible to even recognize the blonde beauty.

“In my dressing room. Need some motivation to get on this treadmill this AM. Got any?” McCarthy wrote as a caption.

McCarthy, of course, has made news over the past few weeks for reasons that have nothing to do with her appearance:

 
  • She clarified her stance on vaccinations.
  • She got engaged to Donnie Wahlberg!

    • We send this couple our best and we applaud McCarthy, along with the following celebrities, for going makeup-free in such a public manner.
