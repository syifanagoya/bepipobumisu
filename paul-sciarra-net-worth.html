What is Paul Sciarra's Net Worth?

Paul Sciarra is an American businessman and entrepreneur who has a net worth of $1.6 billion. Paul Sciarra is best known for being a co-founder of Pinterest. He was a classmate of the scrapbooking app and website's CEO Ben Silbermann at Yale. Sciarra and Silbermann started Cold Brew Labs in 2008 and their first product was the shopping app Tote.

A third co-founder Evan Sharp was introduced and the three launched the first version of Pinterest available for a desktop in 2010. Sciarra left the company in 2012 to work at Andreessen Horowitz. Sciarra still owns a stake in Pinterest which was valued at about $11 billion in 2015 and has 100 million monthly active users.
